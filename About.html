About SK Army Suppliers

We are a leading supplier of badges and medals to the Sri Lanka Army, delivering top-quality products that honor the service of soldiers.

Story of SK Army Suppliers

Founded with a deep respect for the heroes who serve our nation, SK Army Suppliers began as a small initiative to craft and supply military badges for ceremonial events. Over the years,our dedication to quality and authenticity helped us grow into a trusted partner of the Sri Lanka Army. Every product we design is a tribute to those who serve crafted with precision, heritage, and heart.

Goal SK Army Suppliers

To become the most trusted and respected military supplier in Sri Lanka, delivering unmatched quality and timely service while expanding our reach to support all branches of the armed forces and ceremonial units.

Mission of SK Army Suppliers

To honor the bravery and service of the Sri Lanka Armed Forces by providing high-quality military badges, medals, and ceremonial items that symbolize pride, dedication, and excellence.

Vision of SK Army Suppliers

To be a symbol of honor and heritage in every military ceremony across Sri Lanka, recognized for excellence, trust, and national pride.
